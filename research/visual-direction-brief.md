**Light or dark preference:** Open to Designer recommendation based on research findings.

**Visual references:** The reference Instagram accounts listed in the Strategy Brief serve as the starting point. Analyze their visual language and identify patterns worth adopting, adapting, or differentiating from.

---

## Hard Constraints (Non-Negotiable)

- **No religious imagery.** No crosses, halos, hands in prayer, church imagery, scripture-adjacent typography, or any visual language associated with organized religion or spirituality in a religious context. Manifestation and gratitude content must stay grounded visually — nature, light, abstract, and human-potential themes are appropriate.
- No imagery that reads as cheap, low-effort, or stock-photo generic.
- No visual styles that conflict with the tone guardrails in the Strategy Brief (no bro-culture aesthetics, no hyper-masculine color palettes, no gimmicky neon).

---

## Format Priorities

The primary content format is **Carousel Reels** (video with carousel-style frames). The visual system must be built with this in mind:

- Text overlays must be legible on mobile in motion
- Backgrounds must work as video loops (movement is preferred over static where possible)
- The template system should prioritize multi-frame layouts (carousel-style) as the default
