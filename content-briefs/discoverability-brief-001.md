### Caption SEO Guidance

**First line:** "identity shift" belongs in the first line of the caption. Instagram's search algorithm weights the first line most heavily. Do not move it to the body.

**Body lines:** Introduce "who you are becoming" and "self-limiting beliefs" in separate sentences. Do not stack in the same line.

**Final line (CTA):** Save prompt. "Save this when you need a reminder that you are not behind" or structural equivalent. Drives the save signal and embeds the confirmed "you are not behind" search phrase.

**Caption length target:** 100–150 words.

**What to avoid:**
- Do not open with a question.
- Do not use "universe," "vibration," "energy," or any language from the banned list.
- Do not repeat "identity shift" more than once across the caption.
- Do not use "you've got this," "just believe," or any phrase that implies passivity.
